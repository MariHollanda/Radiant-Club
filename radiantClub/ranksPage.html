Ranks

Ranks are Match Making Rank (MMR) that players earn throughout playing Valorant. Ranks can only be earned through the Ranked Mode playlist, Unranked Mode will not earn you any ranks. Abandoning matches will lead to reduced progression and game restrictions. And decisive games where you win many more rounds than your opponents will strongly affect your rank. Very close matches (such as a 13 - 12) will have a smaller impact on your rank.
